Medikaynak mobile application was created by Roche Müstahzarları San. A.Ş. as a resource where healthcare professionals can access up-to-date medical information on various therapeutic areas on a daily basis.

The application includes the following features:

Current Turkish literature summaries

Access to reference articles

Detailed information about congresses

Comprehensive medical information about therapeutic areas, physician videos, literature search

Case consultations with key opinion leader (KOL) physicians

Live training opportunities with KOL physicians

Digital pathology training under the guidance of KOL physicians

Medical calculation tools you may need

Detailed product information (MOA videos, indications, SPC/PI and reimbursement details)

Access to the most up-to-date information on all drugs available in Türkiye (safety precautions, dosage, SPC/PI/reimbursement texts, etc.)

Easy search and access functionality

Thank you for using Medikaynak! We listened to our users’ feedback and made updates to ensure accurate and fast access to medical information.
