"EstatePie offers property managers, landlords and tenants the following services:
- Manage properties and tenants information
- Manage payments and billing respective to their properties
- Dashboard access; varies according to plan type
- Alerts and events notifications
- Messaging between listing owner and interested parties
- Messaging between property owner/ manager and tenants
- Monthly reporting (both manual and automatic)
- Manage schedules for rent, utilities and specified payments
EstatePie aims to provide a clean and uncluttered property management tool providing easy to use and practical features to make your property management seamless and as easy as pie"
