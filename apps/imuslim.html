Key Features

Accurate Prayer Times

Most accurate prayer times including Suhur, Fajr, Zuhr, Asr, Maghrib, Isha, and Ramadan timings with location-based precision.

AR Qibla Compass

World's first Islamic app with Augmented Reality Qibla direction, integrated compass, and distance to Kaaba with prayer mat selection.

Holy Quran

Complete Quran with beautiful recitation, multiple reciters, and easy navigation for spiritual reading and listening.

Tasbeeh Counter

Digital prayer beads with phonetic support in multiple languages, Azkar for before, during, and after prayer.

Islamic Calendar

Complete Hijri calendar with religious holidays, monthly prayer schedules (Imsakiye), and important Islamic dates.

Beautiful Athan

Most beautiful Athan sounds from around the world with full Athan Pro feature, dynamic wallpapers, and Apple Watch integration.
