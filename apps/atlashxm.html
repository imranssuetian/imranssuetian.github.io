AtlasHXM

Comprehensive HR Management Platform

The Atlas mobile app streamlines HR processes and provides easy access to payroll, benefits, time management, and employee onboarding. Whether you're an onboarding candidate or worksite employee, manage your profile, banking, documents, and HR information seamlessly.

Key Features

Employee Management

Comprehensive profile management including personal information, banking details, emergency contacts, and HR documents access.

Payroll & Benefits

Easy access to payroll information, pay slips, benefits management, and banking information updates.

Time Management

Submit time-off requests, manage expenses, clock in/out functionality, and timesheet management for accurate tracking.

Onboarding Process

Complete onboarding workflow for new candidates including document upload, digital signature, and information completion.

Biometric Security

Face identification and fingerprint authentication for enhanced security and ID document verification with selfie matching.

Smart Notifications

Receive notification alerts for important updates, deadlines, and HR-related communications directly on your device.

Technical Implementation

Enterprise-Grade HR Solution

AtlasHXM is built with React Native to ensure seamless cross-platform functionality while maintaining enterprise-level security standards. The app integrates with existing HR systems and provides exceptional support for EOR services with web platform integration.
