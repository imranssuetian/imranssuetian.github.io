Aadios

Personalized Music & Video Streaming App

A custom-built streaming application inspired by YouTube, designed to provide a tailored entertainment experience. Aadios fetches data from YouTube's platform and presents it through a clean, intuitive interface with advanced personalization features for music and video content.

Key Features

Easy Account Creation

Seamless signup process with social login integration for quick access to personalized content and features.

Smart Discovery

Explore recommended artists, top trending content, and personalized genres based on your preferences and listening history.

Playlist Management

Create, edit, and manage custom playlists with easy organization and sharing capabilities for your favorite content.

Radio Streaming

Stream a wide range of radio channels categorized by genre, mood, and popularity for continuous entertainment.

Favorites Library

Access your favorite tracks, playlists, and artists in one convenient hub with quick access and organization.

Premium Subscription

Upgrade to premium plans for an ad-free, enhanced experience with exclusive features and high-quality streaming.

Technical Implementation

YouTube-Powered Streaming Platform

Aadios leverages YouTube's vast content library while providing a customized user experience tailored for music and video streaming. The app implements modern iOS development practices with seamless API integration and personalized content delivery.
